Boracay Island

Famous for its powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters, Boracay is one of the Philippines’ most popular tourist destinations. Whether you’re seeking vibrant nightlife, water adventures, or simply a peaceful walk along White Beach, Boracay has something for everyone.

Puerto Princesa, Palawan
